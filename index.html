Using the App

Adding Feeds: Click the "Manage" button in the sidebar, then click "+ Add Feed" to subscribe to RSS feeds.

Organizing Feeds: Create categories/folders in the Feed Management page to organize your subscriptions.

Reading Articles: Click on a feed in the sidebar to view articles, then click an article to read it in the preview pane.

Image Toggle: Use the image toggle button in the toolbar to show or hide images in article previews.

Keyboard Shortcuts

J - Navigate to next article

K - Navigate to previous article

V - Open selected article in browser

R - Refresh all feeds

[ - Collapse sidebar

] - Expand sidebar

Escape - Close modals

Quality of Life Features

Keyboard Navigation: Quickly navigate through articles using J/K keys for a vim-like experience.

Image Toggle: Toggle images on/off in article previews for faster loading or cleaner reading.

Toast Notifications: Visual feedback for actions like toggling images, with non-intrusive notifications.

Sidebar Toggle: Use [ and ] keys to quickly show or hide the sidebar for more reading space.
